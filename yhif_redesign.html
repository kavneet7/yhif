About YHIF

One of the world's largest youth populations not only needs awareness regarding where their energies can be channelized but also a clear path forward. Every individual - be it a teacher, doctor, student, lawyer, or any other professional - has a critical role in building society.

We all have a leader within us. Let us come together to build a platform not for solving personal initiatives, but to give back to society through a common platform of transformation and growth.

🎯 Our Vision

We seek to create a positive environment where everyone comes with a mindset to give and build together.

🚀 Our Mission

Bringing change through transformation in education, rural development, and skill development across India.
